Frequently asked questions

Whether you're a content creator, brand, or business — we know you have questions. Here are quick answers to help you better understand how our video editing process works and how we can bring your vision to life.

  • What types of videos do you edit?
    We edit a wide range of videos including YouTube videos, Instagram reels, TikToks, promotional brand content, corporate videos, vlogs, podcasts, and more.
  • What’s your typical turnaround time?
    Turnaround time depends on video length and complexity, but we typically deliver within 48–72 hours. Faster delivery options are available on request.
  • How do I send you my footage?
    You can upload your raw clips via Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. Once received, we’ll confirm and begin editing your video.
  • Can I request changes after delivery?
    Absolutely! Every project comes with 1–2 free revision rounds to make sure you’re fully satisfied with the final video.
  • Do you offer subscription plans or bulk packages?
    Yes, we offer flexible plans for recurring video content — perfect for YouTubers, agencies, or brands producing videos regularly.
